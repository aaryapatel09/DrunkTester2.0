Not a breathalyzer. This is a research toy that measures three proxies correlated with impairment — reaction time, gaze stability, and speech smoothness — and compares them against a sober baseline you record first. It cannot measure blood alcohol content. Do not use it to decide whether to drive.

DrunkTester 2.0

Three short tests in your browser. Nothing is uploaded — everything runs client-side on this page.

Reaction time

5 trials. When the circle turns green, click as fast as you can. We measure your mean RT and its variability (SD). Alcohol raises both.

Click to start
2

Gaze stability

Stare at the centre dot for 10 seconds. We track your irises via MediaPipe and measure how much they drift. Intoxication reduces gaze steadiness (nystagmus, saccade overshoot).

Click to start camera
3

Speech smoothness

Read the sentence aloud. We transcribe via the browser's Web Speech API and measure the word-error rate plus how long it took. Slurred or hesitant speech pushes both up.

Composite impairment score
